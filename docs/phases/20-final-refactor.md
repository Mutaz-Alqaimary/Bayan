# Phase 20 — Final Refactor

## Goal
Step back and review the whole platform honestly.

## Build
- `ProjectReview.md` covering: strengths, weaknesses, future improvements, technical decisions,
  scaling strategy
- Final architecture review
- Production readiness checklist
- Deployment readiness checklist

## Definition of done
- `ProjectReview.md` is honest about weaknesses and known gaps, not just a strengths list.
- Production readiness checklist cross-references the Phase 17 security review and Phase 15
  accessibility audit rather than re-deriving them from scratch.
- Scaling strategy addresses what happens as schools/students/passages grow — query patterns,
  pagination limits, caching — grounded in what was actually built, not generic advice.
